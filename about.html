BAND BOX PRODUCTIONS CELEBRATES 25 YEARS

About Us

M/S BAND BOX PRODUCTIONS is a Film, Video & Photography Production house established in 1997 and involved in verticals of producing Corporate Films, AVs, Music Videos & promos, TV Programmes, Documentaries, TV Spots, Social & Public service films and Licensing of Indian content to Networks around the world.

We have produced Corporate films for Tata Motors, Airtel, Toshiba, Videocon, Mckinseys, GE, Reliance Communications, Coca Cola, Thomas Cook, Citi Bank, Novartis, Dr.Reddys, JnJ, Sanofi, Roche Diagnostics and Taj Hotels to name a few.

We are the pioneers of the Music Promo (Packaged &3D/2D Animated TV Ad Spots for music albums), having produced over 5000 promos for various Retro and current Indian artistes, International singers and bands. We have to our credit the TV ad spots of some of the highest selling Devotional, Classical and Ghazal Albums in India (e.g. Hey Ram by Jagit Singh).

Our Music Industry clients include most Indian Music labels namely Saregama HMV, Sony, BMG, Warner Music, EMI Music, Virgin Music, Universal Music, Tips, T Series, Magnasound and Crescendo Music. Band Box has Produced promotional Music Videos for Indian artistes and Bollywood films like Bombay Vikings (Neeraj Sridhar), Silk Route (Mohit Chauhan), Sukhbir, Lata Mangeshkar, Nazia Hassan, Khosla Ka Ghosla among many others.

We have produced TV promos, Theatrical Trailers, and behind the Scenes for Hindi Films. We at Band Box have also produced Documentaries, social and public awareness films for UNICEF. E.g. we have shot some of “The Pulse Polio” campaigns with various film celebrities. Band Box Productions is one of the largest overseas content providers for “Ripley’s Believe it or Not”, a TV programme produced by Sony in the USA and shown in over 100 countries. We have researched, produced and executed over 50 episode segments.

Currently, Band Box Productions is providing various Audio /Visual, Video, animation and Photography production services to a host of Pharmaceutical and Healthcare related organisations like Novartis, Sanofi, J&J , Merck, Roche Diagnostics, Santen and Koolex Cold Chain to name a few.
